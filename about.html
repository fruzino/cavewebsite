About Cave

How it started, formed and now what its known for.

Cave is a programming language that is aimed towards beginners who want a taste of C and Python

Cave is a simple programming language that is easy to learn and use.

The History of Cave

Cave was born in early 2026 (March) as a reborn version of C

Cave was named after the developer's favorite Marvel character 'Iron Man'. If you turn C upside down you get a symbol that sort of resembles a Cave.

The developer, made Cave as his previous programming language 'Mint' failed due to errors and lack of proper plan. The developer also wanted C to be much more easier and wanted to help beginners get a taste of C and python at the same time.
